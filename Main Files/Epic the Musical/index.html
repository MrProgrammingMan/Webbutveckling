Epic: The Musical

Epic: The Musical, is a musical that is based on Homer's 'Odyssey' and is written and composed by Jorge Rivera-Herrans, he plays Odysseus and is the character we follow on his journey home.

There are 9 sagas in total, with each saga including between 3-5 songs, these all cover different parts of the Odyssey, sometimes throwing in their own information different from what actually happened in the Odyssey.

Here is a list of the sagas along with a song in it: The Troy Saga The Cyclops Saga The Ocean Saga The Circe Saga The Underworld Saga The Thunder Saga The Wisdom Saga The Vengeance Saga The Ithica Saga
